Who We Are

Bridgehaven Capital Management was founded in 2019 by our Managing Partner, Jed Otteson. With a BA in Finance from Gustavus Adolphus College (2000), Jed brings over 15 years of experience as a Senior Equity Trader at Whitebox Advisors, complemented by equity trading and support roles at Piper Jaffray and Deephaven Capital Management. His expertise in tactical asset allocation drives our client-focused, fee-only RIA in Excelsior, Minnesota, delivering transparent, personalized financial solutions to help you achieve your goals.
